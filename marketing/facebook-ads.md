## ⚠️ Compliance — read before you spend a dollar

1. **Meta requires authorization for gambling/betting ads.** Even though MLB Edge sells *analysis* (not bets), Meta's reviewers often lump picks/handicapping in with gambling. Expect possible rejection. To reduce risk:
   - Frame everything as **information/entertainment**, never as a way to make money.
   - Never show cash, payouts, dollar winnings, or "guaranteed"/"lock"/"can't lose" language.
   - Keep 21+ and responsible-gaming text on the creative and in the copy.
2. **No income or win-rate promises.** Do not add "62% win rate", "+ROI", or testimonials unless they are real, current, and documented — and even then Meta may reject them. Safer to omit entirely in ads.
3. **Targeting:** exclude under-21 automatically by setting the ad audience age minimum to 21+.
4. **If an ad is rejected:** don't resubmit the same creative repeatedly (hurts account standing). Adjust toward softer, more "entertainment/analysis" framing and request review once.
5. **Disclosure:** because this is a paid product, your landing page must clearly state what's free vs paid (it does) and your refund/terms links must be reachable (they are in the footer).
